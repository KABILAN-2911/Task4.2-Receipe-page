Grandma's Secret Applie pie


This apple pie receipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to digest.


Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cannamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degree Fahrenheit
  2. In a bowl, milk the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
